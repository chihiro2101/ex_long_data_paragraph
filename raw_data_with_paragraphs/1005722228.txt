1992 in literature

==Events==
*August – An attempt is made to set fire to the National Library of Abkhazia in Sukhumi during the War in Abkhazia by Georgian forces.
*August 25 – The National and University Library of Bosnia and Herzegovina is annihilated during the Siege of Sarajevo by the Army of Republika Srpska.<ref></ref>
*September – Michael Ondaatje's historiographic metafiction The English Patient is published in Canada. It will win The Golden Man Booker in 2018.
*unknown date – The Goosebumps series of children's horror fiction, penned by R. L. Stine, is first published in the United States.<ref></ref>
