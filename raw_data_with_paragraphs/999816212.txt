==Television ratings==
17.3 million viewers watched the 1995 Grammy Awards.
==Notes==

The 37th Annual Grammy Awards were presented on March 1, 1995, at Shrine Auditorium, Los Angeles. They recognized accomplishments by musicians from the previous year.<ref></ref><ref></ref> Bruce Springsteen was the night's biggest winner with 4 awards, including Song of the Year while opening the show with his Grammy nominated hit.
