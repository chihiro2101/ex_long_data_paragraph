Seymour is a census-designated place (CDP) and unincorporated community in Blount and Sevier counties in the U.S. state of Tennessee. The CDP population was 10,919 at the 2010 U.S. census.<ref>U.S. Census Bureau, urltoken QuickFacts - Seymour, Tennessee . Retrieved: February 7, 2012.</ref> It is included in the Knoxville, Tennessee Knoxville metropolitan area.
