The Ruling Class is a 1972 British black comedy film.<ref>Variety film review; 19 April 1972, page 18.</ref> It is an adaptation of Peter Barnes' satire stage play The Ruling Class which tells the story of a paranoid schizophrenia British nobleman (played by Peter O'Toole) who inherits a peerage. The film co-stars Alastair Sim, William Mervyn, Coral Browne, Harry Andrews, Carolyn Seymour, James Villiers and Arthur Lowe. It was produced by Jules Buck and directed by Peter Medak.
The film has been described as a "commercial failure&nbsp;. that has since become a cult classic";<ref name="tcm"/> Peter O'Toole described it as "a comedy with tragic relief".<ref name="news.independent.co.uk"></ref>
