Éleuthère Irénée du Pont de Nemours ;<ref> urltoken "DuPont". Random House Webster's Unabridged Dictionary.</ref> ; 24 June 1771 – 31 October 1834) was a French-American chemist and industrialist who founded the gunpowder manufacturer DuPont (1802–2017). His descendants, the du Pont family, have been one of America's richest and most prominent families since the 19th century, with generations of influential businessmen, politicians and philanthropists.
