Chabad, also known as Lubavitch, Habad and Chabad-Lubavitch<ref>Additional spellings include Lubawitz, and Jabad (in Spanish speaking countries)</ref> , is an Orthodox Jewish Hasidic dynasty. Chabad is one of the world's best-known Hasidic movements, particularly for its outreach activities. It is one of the largest Hasidic groups<ref></ref> and Jewish religious organizations in the world.
