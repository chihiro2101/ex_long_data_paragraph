The Feldenkrais Method is a type of exercise therapy<ref name=ehm/> devised by Israeli Moshé Feldenkrais (1904–1984) during the mid-20th century. The method is claimed to reorganize connections between the brain and body and so improve body movement and psychological state.<ref name="ehm" />
There is no good medical evidence that the Feldenkrais method confers any health benefits. It is not known if it is safe or cost-effective,<ref name="aus17" /> but researchers do not believe it poses serious risks.<ref name="Corgi"></ref>
