Causeway Bay is a city of Hong Kong, located on Hong Kong Island, and covering parts of Wan Chai District. The Cantonese name is also romanised as Tung Lo Wan as in Tung Lo Wan Road. The rents in the shopping areas of Causeway Bay were ranked as the world's most expensive for the second year in a row, after overtaking New York City's Fifth Avenue in 2012.<ref>Straus, Karsten. urltoken "Hong Kong Beats New York For Chic Shopping ", Forbes, 21 November 2013.</ref>
