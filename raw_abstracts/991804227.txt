The combat occurred near Tudela, Navarre, Spain during the Peninsular War, part of a wider conflict known as the Napoleonic Wars.
Spanish casualties were estimated to be about 4,000 dead and 3,000 prisoners out of a total force of 33,000. The French, in all, lost no more than 650 dead and wounded out of a total of 31,000.
