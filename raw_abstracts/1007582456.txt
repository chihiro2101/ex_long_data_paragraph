Jičín ; , Gitschin or Jitschin; , Gitzinum;<ref> urltoken "G", Johann Georg Theodor Grässe, Orbis Latinus, 1861, 2nd ed. Berlin: Schmidt, 1909, </ref> is a town in the Hradec Králové Region of the Czech Republic. It has about 17,000 inhabitants. It lies approximately 85&nbsp;km northeast of Prague in the scenic region of the Bohemian Paradise under the Prachov Rocks.
The Battle of Gitschin was fought nearby during the Austro-Prussian War of 1866.
