Zhang Liao accompanied Lü Bu as they headed east and wandered around central and northern China until mid 195, when the warlord Liu Bei offered Lü Bu shelter in Xu Province. In 196, Lü Bu betrayed his host and seized control of Xu Province, after which he appointed a 27-year-old Zhang Liao as the Chancellor of Lu State (魯國; around present-day Qufu, Shandong).<ref name="Zhang Liao was 28">(布為李傕所敗，從布東奔徐州，領魯相，時年二十八。) Sanguozhi vol. 17.</ref>
