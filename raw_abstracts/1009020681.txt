The flag of North Korea, also known as the Ramhongsaek Konghwagukgi ; literally "blue and red-colored flag of the republic"), consists of a central red panel, bordered both above and below by a narrow white stripe and a broad blue stripe.<ref></ref> The central red panel bears a five-pointed red star within a white circle near the hoist.<ref name=Constitution/> The flag is banned from public use in South Korea due to its association with the ruling North Korean regime, although some exceptions for the usage of the flag exist.<ref> urltoken
