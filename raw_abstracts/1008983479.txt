Comedy Central is an American basic cable channel owned by ViacomCBS through its domestic network division's MTV Entertainment Group unit, based in Manhattan. The channel is geared for mature audiences and carries comedy programming in the form of both original, licensed, and syndicated series, stand-up comedy specials, and feature films. It is available to approximately 86.723&nbsp;million households in the United States as of September 2018.<ref></ref>
Since the early 2000s, Comedy Central has expanded globally with localized channels in Europe, India, Southeast Asia, Latin America, Australia and New Zealand,<ref name="Yle News"></ref><ref name="hollywoodreporter1"></ref> Middle East, and Africa.<ref name="comedycentralafrica.com"></ref> The international channels are operated by ViacomCBS Networks International.
