Diborane(6), generally known as diborane, is the chemical compound consisting of boron and hydrogen with the formula B<sub>2</sub>H<sub>6</sub>. It is a colorless, pyrophoric gas with a repulsively sweet odor. Synonyms include boroethane, boron hydride, and diboron hexahydride. Diborane is a key boron compound with a variety of applications. It has attracted wide attention for its electronic structure. Its derivatives are useful reagents.
