Prunella Margaret Rumney West Scales<ref>The International Who's Who, 1997–98, Europa Publications p. 1332</ref><ref>The International Who's Who of Women 2002, 3rd edition, ed. Elizabeth Sleeman, Europa Publications, 2001, p. 510</ref> (née Illingworth; born 22 June 1932) is an English actress and presenter best known for her role as Basil Fawlty's wife Sybil in the BBC comedy Fawlty Towers and her BAFTA award-nominated role as Queen Elizabeth II in A Question of Attribution (Screen One, BBC 1991) by Alan Bennett.<ref></ref>
