Ingeborg Hermine Morath ; May 27, 1923 &ndash; January 30, 2002) was an Austrian-born American photographer.<ref name=obit/> In 1953, she joined the Magnum Photos Agency, founded by top photographers in Paris, and became a full photographer with the agency in 1955. Morath was also the third wife of playwright Arthur Miller; their daughter is screenwriter/director Rebecca Miller.
