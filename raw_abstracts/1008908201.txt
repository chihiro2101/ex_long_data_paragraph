The metric book was brought into attention by V. Ya. Chernushev, the birth date of Zworykin was revised by K. M. Zworykin invented a television transmitting and receiving system employing cathode ray tubes. He played a role in the practical development of television from the early thirties, including charge storage-type tubes, infrared image tubes and the electron microscope.<ref></ref>
