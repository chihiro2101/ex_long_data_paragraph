Yahoo! , styled as yahoo!)<ref></ref><ref></ref> is an American web services provider. It is headquartered in Sunnyvale, California and owned by Verizon Media, which acquired it in 2017 for $4.48 billion.<ref name="Yahoo!-Inc-Feb-2017-8-K"></ref><ref name="Yahoo!-Inc-Jun-2017-SC-TO-I/A"></ref><ref></ref><ref name="Yahoo!-Inc-May-2017-SC-TO-I"></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>
It provides a web portal, search engine Yahoo! Search, and related services, including Yahoo! Mail, Yahoo! News, Yahoo! Finance, Yahoo! Answers, Yahoo! Maps, Yahoo! Video, fantasy sports, advertising, and its social media website.
Yahoo! was founded by Jerry Yang and David Filo in January 1994 and was incorporated on March 2, 1995.<ref></ref><ref></ref> Yahoo was one of the pioneers of the early Internet era in the 1990s.<ref></ref> In 2000, it was the most popular website.<ref></ref> Usage slowly declined as it lost market share to Google.<ref name="telegraph.co.uk"></ref><ref></ref> However, Yahoo domain websites are still among the most popular websites, ranking 11th in global engagement according to Alexa Internet<ref></ref> and 10th according to SimilarWeb.<ref></ref>
