John Shelby "Jack" Spong (born June 16, 1931) is a retired American bishop of the Episcopal Church. From 1979 to 2000, he was the Bishop of Newark, New Jersey. A liberal Christian Theology, religion commentator and author, he calls for a fundamental rethinking of Christian belief away from theism and traditional doctrines.<ref name=ABC1> urltoken Interview. ABC Radio Australia, June 17, 2001</ref>
