Bwana Devil is a 1952 U.S. adventure B movie written, directed, and produced by Arch Oboler, and starring Robert Stack, Barbara Britton, and Nigel Bruce.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref name=Sala></ref> Bwana Devil is based on the true story of the Tsavo maneaters and filmed with the Natural Vision 3D system.<ref name=Sala/> The film is notable for sparking the first 3D film craze in the motion picture industry, as well as for being the first feature-length 3D film in color and the first 3D sound feature in English.
The advertising tagline was: The Miracle of the Age!!! A LION in your lap! A LOVER in your arms!
