Real McCoy is a German Eurodance and pop music project best known for hit singles "Another Night",<ref name="Allmusic.com Biography on Real McCoy"></ref> "Automatic Lover (Call for Love)", "Run Away",<ref name="Billboard Magazine Mar 18, 1995"></ref> "Love & Devotion", "Come and Get Your Love", "One More Time" and the multi-platinum album Another Night.<ref name="Billboard Magazine May 13, 1995"></ref>
