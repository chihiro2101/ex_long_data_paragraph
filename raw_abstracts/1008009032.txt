* 2 November 2006: Spacecraft suffers an error while attempting to reorient a solar panel and communication was lost.
* 5 November 2006: Weak signals were detected, indicating the spacecraft was awaiting instructions. The signal cut out later that day.<ref name="loss of contact" />
