Vice Admiral Sir George Carteret, 1st Baronet 161018 January 1680 N.S.) was a royalist statesman in Jersey and England, who served in the Clarendon ministry as Treasurer of the Navy. He was also one of the original lord proprietor of the former British colony of Carolina and New Jersey. Carteret, New Jersey, as well as Carteret County, North Carolina, both in the United States, are named after him.
