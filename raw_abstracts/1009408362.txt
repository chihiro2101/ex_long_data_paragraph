Modern enka, as developed in the postwar era, is a form of Sentimental_ballad. Some of the first modern enka singers were Hachiro Kasuga, Michiya Mihashi, and Hideo Murata.<ref name="ntv"></ref> The revival of enka in its modern form is said to date from 1969, when Keiko Fuji made her debut. The most famous male enka singers are Shinichi Mori and Kiyoshi Hikawa.
