Brigitte Fontaine, (born 24 June 1939) is a singer of avant-garde music.<ref>Gordons, Kim. "Music Avant-Garde—Brigitte Fontaine" Interview 1 October 2001</ref><ref>Price, Simon. "Only Connect: The spirit of Serge is alive and smoking" 29 October 2006, The Independent.</ref> She has employed numerous unusual musical styles, melding rock and roll, folk, jazz, electronica, spoken word poetry, and world. She has collaborated with Stereolab, Michel Colombier, Jean-Claude Vannier, Areski Belkacem, Gotan Project, Sonic Youth, Antoine Duhamel, Grace Jones, Noir Désir, Archie Shepp, Arno, and The Art Ensemble of Chicago.
She is also a novelist, playwright, poet, and actress.
