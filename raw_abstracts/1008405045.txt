The Beothuk or ; also spelled Beothuck)<ref></ref><ref></ref> were a group of indigenous people who lived on the island of Newfoundland.<ref></ref>
Beginning around AD 1500, the Beothuk culture formed. This appeared to be the most recent cultural manifestation of peoples who first migrated from Labrador to present-day Newfoundland around AD 1.
