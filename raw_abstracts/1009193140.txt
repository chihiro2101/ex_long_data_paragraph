The burbot (Lota lota) is the only gadiform (cod-like) freshwater fish. It is also known as bubbot,<ref></ref> mariah, freshwater cod,<ref name=guardian2020 /> freshwater ling, freshwater cusk, the lawyer, coney-fish, lingcod,<ref name=SOED3></ref> and eelpout. The species is closely related to the marine common ling and the cusk. It is the monotypic of the genus Lota. For some time of the year, the burbot lives under ice, and it requires frigid temperatures to breed.<ref></ref>
