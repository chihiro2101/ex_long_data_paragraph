Girolamo Fracastoro ; c. 1476/86 August 1553<ref>The date given in the Vita is "octavo idus Augusti MDLIII." (In: Opera omnia, 1555; 1574; urltoken 1584); for conversion see e.g. here.</ref>) was an Italian physician, poet, and scholar in mathematics, geography and astronomy.<ref></ref> Fracastoro subscribed to the philosophy of atomism, and rejected appeals to hidden causes in scientific investigation. His studies of the mode of syphilis transmission are an early example of epidemiology.
