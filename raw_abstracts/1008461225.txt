Mutts is a daily comic strip created by Patrick McDonnell on September 5, 1994.<ref name=Holtz></ref> Distributed by King Features Syndicate, it follows the adventures of Earl, a dog, and Mooch, a cat. Earl and Mooch interact with each other, their human owners, and also the animals around their neighborhood.
Peanuts creator Charles Schulz praised Mutts, calling it "one of the best comic strips of all time."<ref></ref>
