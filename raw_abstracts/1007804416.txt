The automotive industry comprises a wide range of company and organizations involved in the design, development, manufacturing, marketing, and selling of motor vehicles.<ref name=":0"></ref> It is one of the world's largest industries by revenue. The automotive industry does not include industries dedicated to the maintenance of Car following delivery to the end-user, such as Automobile repair shop and motor fuel filling stations.
