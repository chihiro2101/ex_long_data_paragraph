ABB, formerly ASEA Brown Boveri, is a Sweden-Swiss multinational corporation headquartered in Zürich, Switzerland,<ref></ref> operating mainly in robotics, power, heavy electrical equipment, and automation technology areas. It is ranked 341st in the Fortune Global 500 list of 2018 and has been a global Fortune 500 company for 24 years.<ref></ref> Until the sale of its electricity division in 2020, ABB was Switzerland's largest industrial employer.<ref></ref> ABB is traded on the SIX Swiss Exchange in Zürich, Nasdaq Nordic and the New York Stock Exchange in the United States.<ref></ref>
