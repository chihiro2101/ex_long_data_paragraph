Martin Carthy MBE (born 21 May 1941) is an English folk singer and guitarist who has remained one of the most influential figures in Folk music of England, inspiring contemporaries such as Bob Dylan and Paul Simon,<ref name="urlSignOnSanDiego.com | The San Diego Union-Tribune | San Diego Green Guide"></ref> and later artists such as Richard Thompson, since he emerged as a young musician in the early days of the folk revival.
