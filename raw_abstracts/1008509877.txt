Rotselaar is a municipality located in the Belgium province of Flemish Brabant, near the convergence of the Demer and the Dijle. Since January 1, 1977 the municipality comprises the towns of Rotselaar proper, Werchter and Wezemaal. On January 1, 2006, Rotselaar had a total population of 15,068. The total area is 37.57&nbsp;km² which gives a population density of 401 inhabitants per km².
