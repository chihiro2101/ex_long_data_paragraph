London Metropolitan University, commonly known as London Met, is a public research university in London, England. The University of North London (formerly the Polytechnic of North London) and London Guildhall University (formerly the City of London Polytechnic) merged in 2002 to create the university.<ref></ref><ref name="TheIndependentAZ"></ref> The University's roots go back to 1848. 
The university has campuses in the City of London and in the London Borough of Islington, a museum, archives and libraries. Special collections include the TUC Library,<ref></ref> the Irish Studies Collection and the Frederick Parker Collection.<ref> urltoken The Frederick Parker Collection </ref>
