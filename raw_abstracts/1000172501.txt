Abul-Abbas was an Asian elephant brought back to Carolingian Empire Charlemagne by his diplomat Isaac the Jew. The gift was from the Abbasid caliph Harun al-Rashid and symbolizes the beginning of Abbasidâ€“Carolingian alliance.
