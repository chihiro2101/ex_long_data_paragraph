Barbara Ehrenreich , ;<ref></ref> born August 26, 1941) is an American author and activism who describes herself as "a myth buster by trade"<ref name=booknotes></ref> and has been called "a veteran muckraker" by The New Yorker.<ref></ref> During the 1980s and early 1990s she was a prominent figure in the Democratic Socialists of America. She is a widely read and award-winning columnist and essayist, and author of 21 books. Ehrenreich is perhaps best known for her 2001 book Nickel and Dimed.
